## PROMPTS PARA IA DE VÍDEO

### CENA 1 — Abertura cinematic
```
Cinematic slow-motion shot of a modern barbershop interior at night. Warm golden lighting from edison bulbs. Dark leather chairs, mirrors reflecting light. Camera slowly dollies forward through the empty shop. No people. Moody atmosphere, shallow depth of field. 4K, film grain.
```

### CENA 2 — Cliente indeciso
```
Medium shot of a young Brazilian man sitting in a barber chair, looking at his phone with a confused expression. He shows the phone screen to the barber standing behind him. Modern barbershop background with dark walls and warm lighting. Natural skin tones, realistic. Cinematic color grading, shallow DOF. 4K.
```

### CENA 3 — Barbeiro frustrado
```
Close-up of a barber's hands holding scissors, hesitating. Cut to the barber's face looking uncertain, glancing between the client's head and a phone propped up on the mirror showing a celebrity photo. Modern barbershop. Warm tones, cinematic. 4K.
```

### CENA 4 — Cliente usando o totem
```
Over-the-shoulder shot of a young man standing in front of a sleek wall-mounted tablet in a barbershop waiting area. He touches the screen with his finger. The screen glows with warm golden UI elements on dark background. Modern minimalist barbershop interior. Soft bokeh background. Cinematic, 4K.
```

### CENA 5 — Reação positiva ao resultado da IA
```
Close-up reaction shot of a young Brazilian man smiling and nodding approvingly while looking at a tablet screen. His eyes light up. Barbershop interior background with warm lighting. Natural expression, genuine surprise turning to satisfaction. Cinematic shallow DOF, 4K.
```

### CENA 6 — Cliente pagando via QR code
```
Medium shot of a man's hands holding a smartphone, pointing the camera at a large QR code displayed on a tablet mounted on a barbershop wall. The tablet has a dark interface with a white QR code in the center and golden accent text. Clean modern barbershop. Natural lighting. 4K, cinematic.
```

### CENA 7 — Barbeiro no painel
```
Over-the-shoulder shot of a barber using a laptop at a reception desk in a barbershop. The screen shows a dark-themed dashboard with charts and client cards (blurred). He scrolls confidently. Modern barbershop office area. Warm lighting. Professional atmosphere. 4K, shallow DOF.
```

### CENA 8 — Encerramento
```
Slow cinematic pull-back shot of a modern barbershop exterior at night. Neon sign glowing warmly. Through the glass window we see happy clients and barbers inside. City street reflection in the glass. Moody cinematic tones, anamorphic lens flare. 4K.
```
